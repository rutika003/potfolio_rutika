Role of Digital Reminder Systems in Improving Personal Healthcare Habits.

Research on how reminder systems improve healthcare habits like medication adherence, hydration and appointments.

Read More

As healthcare systems increasingly shift toward preventive care and chronic disease management, Digital Reminder Systems (DRSs) have emerged as scalable technological interventions to improve adherence to personal health behaviors. This study evaluates the effectiveness of digital reminders in enhancing medication adherence, hydration practices, and physical activity. A sequential explanatory mixed-methods approach was adopted, combining evidence synthesized from more than 40 peer-reviewed clinical and mHealth studies with primary survey data collected from 200 users. The findings indicate a substantial personalization effect, with context-aware reminders achieving an 82.5% response rate compared to 55.3% for non-personalized notifications. A marked decline in engagement was observed between Days 14–21, suggesting the onset of notification fatigue. Furthermore, the inclusion of gamification mechanisms, such as streak tracking, was associated with a 30% increase in long-term retention. The study concludes that DRS effectiveness is strongly influenced by personalization, adaptive timing, and the gradual reduction of prompts as behaviors transition toward automaticity. These findings offer evidence-based design implications for the development of sustainable and user-centered digital health interventions.

Monitoring Systems and Techniques for Precision Agriculture

Study of Precision Agriculture by using Monitoring systems and techniques that helps or provide better harvesting of crop yields.

Read More

Precision agriculture leverages advanced monitoring systems and techniques. The aim of the research paper is to provide better techniques, Technologies and methodologies to provide better harvesting of Crop yields, optimise Resource utilization and ensure sustainable farming practices. This paper focus says on how modern technology like Iot sensors, satellite and artificial intelligence will help in changing the way farming works. Instead of relying on guesswork, farmers can now use real time data from the soil, weather conditions and crop health to make better decisions. By integrating real-time data collections and predictive analytics, farmers can make informed decision regarding irrigation, fertilization and pest control.The study also highlights various monitoring techniques such as satellite imaging, drone surveillance and soil nutrient analysis. The adoption of these techniques improves efficiency reduces, environmental impact and supports Global food security. The various artificial intelligence or machine learning algorithms that we used in this research paper are Artificial Neural Networks (ANN), Recurrent Neural Networks (RNN), Naive Bayes, etc. to predict the climate, weather, soil conditions, and plant disease detection, crop monitoring etc. Combining block chain with AI will make farming more transparent, secure and efficient. This research highlights the transformative role of AI and block chain in Precision agriculture, ensuring efficiency, transparency and sustainability. Future advancement in this technologies will create a farming system that will be more reliable, transparent and sustainable, benefiting both farmers and consumer.
